Privacy Policy

one minute lat3r is a first-party publishing tool operated by Redvapor Studio, used only by the studio itself to publish original short films to the studio's own social media accounts (including TikTok account @oneminutelat3r).

  • The tool has no third-party users and collects no personal data from the public.
  • TikTok OAuth tokens are used solely to upload the studio's own videos and read the studio's own post analytics, and are stored locally on studio-owned hardware.
  • No data is sold, shared, or transferred to any third party.
  • Data obtained from TikTok APIs is used only for the studio's own account management and is never combined with other data sources.
